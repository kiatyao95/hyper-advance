01 — Who We Are

About Us

Three decades of precision engineering and system integration across Malaysia's most demanding environments.

Hyper Advance Sdn Bhd

HYPER ADVANCE SDN BHD started operation more than 29 years ago in 1995 with a single staff. Today, HYPER ADVANCE and its group of companies have a staff strength of more than 50 staff. As a distributor of electrical products and system integrators, HYPER ADVANCE has grown from strength to strength and, today is recognised as a leading solution provider in the commercial complexes, low and high-rise residential or non-residential buildings, hospitality and healthcare facilities segments.

We strive and embrace the maxim: "IF IT WEREN'T FOR YOU, WE WOULDN'T BE HERE."

HYPER ADVANCE's mission is to enhance the positioning of our business partners by providing them with state-of-the-art solutions and to ensure a win-win-win situation for us, business partners and end-users in enriching lives for a better world to live in.

To ensure success in our mission: close rapport, team building, knowledge and skills acquisitions, self-renewal and re-learning are key — both within HYPER ADVANCE and with our principals and business partners. We distribute Aiphone, AJB, Fagor, Amperes, Lutron, Austco, Esa Grimma, and Bodet.

HA Meditech Sdn Bhd

HA MEDITECH SDN BHD was incorporated in 2011, initially named as HIM Technology Sdn Bhd, by independent professionals with the aim of exploring opportunities in the healthcare segment. Backed by resources from Hyper Advance and a team with more than 25 years of accumulated healthcare experience, HA Meditech is committed to patient care and patient safety — delivering nurse call, clinical communication, and hospital ELV systems with precision.
